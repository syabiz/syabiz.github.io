What is Dorkcoin?

At its heart, Dorkcoin is a playful memecoin created for the internet’s proud misfits — the Real Dorks. It runs on its own blockchain and uses a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus algorithm rather than traditional Proof-of-Work mining, meaning the network is secured by participants who stake their coins and help keep the system running efficiently.

More than just a coin, Dorkcoin celebrates a fun and inclusive culture where curiosity, creativity, and unapologetic dorkiness are embraced. Built for Real Dorks everywhere, it’s a community-driven project that mixes humor with the spirit of decentralization and the ever-evolving world of crypto.
